Privacy Policy

Certisimo PDE

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Certisimo PDE ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Certisimo PDE is designed with privacy in mind:

  • All your progress and statistics are stored locally on your device
  • We do not require account registration
  • We do not collect names, emails, or any identifying information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Data Storage

All app data, including:

  • Your quiz progress
  • Test scores and statistics
  • Session history
  • App settings

...is stored exclusively on your device using iOS's built-in secure storage. This data is never transmitted to external servers.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset Progress" option in the app's Settings
  • Deleting the app from your device

Third-Party Services

The app may contain links to official Google Cloud documentation. When you tap these links, you will be directed to external websites governed by their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for professionals and students preparing for certification exams.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
